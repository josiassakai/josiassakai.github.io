Community Service

In 2014, I had the opportunity of traveling to the Dominican Republic to help out in its communities. My group was composed of 5 adult leaders and 15 high school juniors. Over the nine-day trip, we performed tasks of landscaping, painting, construction, public speaking, and children's ministry. During the time I spent there, I made some very good friends and some unforgettable memories. Overall, the experience was both exhausting and incredibly rewarding.
