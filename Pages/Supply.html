Power Supply

While working on this project, I gained a more thorough understanding of how each part of a power supply circuit operated. Some of these included the transformer, rectifier circuit, and filter capacitors. It was my first time using a milling machine to print a circuit board, so there was a bit of a learning curve that I had to adjust to. After rigorous testing of prototypes for bad connections, in the end I was grateful for the room saved in the project box that all the parts were going in. My favorite part of working on this power supply was designing the project box and figuring out the best way to lay out both the internal and external components. Having all the parts come together neatly and securely was very satisfying. The end result was a 30V variable DC power supply that was used to power many kits. It saved countless batteries from premature deaths.
