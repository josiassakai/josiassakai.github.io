Audio Amplifier

Working on the amplifier was interesting because I got to revisit a lot of design steps used in constructing my power supply. In some ways, that made this project easier. For example, I now had some more experience with parts like the transformer and ac plug. I had also worked with the same exact project box, so modifying it to my liking went more smoothly this time around. However, some of the challenges I faced with my variable power supply were also present while working on the amplifier. The best example of this was having to design yet another printed circuit board, and a slightly more crowded one at that. Having less external components was nice though, as that meant less holes to put through metal plates. The finished product has been a staple of my home entertainment system since the day it was completed.
